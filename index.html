What We Supply

Two products.
Two industries.

We supply densified and undensified silica fume — a pozzolanic admixture that dramatically increases the strength, durability, and chemical resistance of concrete and well cement.

Construction

Densified Silica Fume

Pellets — Construction

Concentrated pellet form for easy handling and batching at ready-mix plants. Extends concrete service life to 100+ years for bridges, highways, marine structures, and high-performance applications.

Bulk Pneumatic Super Sacks Palletized Bags

Undensified Silica Fume

Powder — Oilfield

Raw powder form for well cementing and drilling fluid applications. Improves well cement integrity, compressive strength, and resistance to downhole chemical attack in oil and gas operations.

Markets We Serve

Built for the
toughest environments.

From highrise foundations to 3,000-metre well bores — our silica fume performs where it matters most.

01 — Construction

Infrastructure That Lasts Generations

Silica fume-enhanced concrete achieves compressive strengths exceeding 100 MPa and dramatically reduces permeability — extending the service life of structures to 100+ years, even in harsh marine and freeze-thaw environments.

Bridges & Highways Marine Structures Parking Structures High-Rise Foundations Shotcrete HPC / UHPC
02 — Oilfield

Well Integrity Under Pressure

In oil and gas cementing, silica fume increases compressive strength, reduces permeability, and resists chemical attack from formation fluids — critical for well integrity at depth and temperature.
